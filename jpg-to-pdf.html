Frequently Asked Questions

Is converting JPG to PDF really free?

Yes. BuildPDF is 100% free with no hidden charges or premium tier. We show ads to keep the service running — that's it.

Does my JPG get uploaded to a server?

No. All conversion happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your image data never leaves your device. It's one of BuildPDF's core privacy guarantees.

Can I convert multiple JPG files into one PDF?

Yes! Select multiple JPEG files and they'll be combined into a single PDF in the order you add them. Perfect for combining photo scans into one document.

What's the maximum file size I can convert?

There's no hard limit, but very large files (above 100 MB) may slow down depending on your device's processing power. Most photos and scans convert in under 2 seconds.

Will converting a JPG to PDF reduce image quality?

By default, BuildPDF uses 95% JPEG quality — virtually indistinguishable from the original. You can also choose Medium (80%) or Low (60%) to reduce file size if needed.

What page size does the PDF use?

You can choose A4, US Letter, A3, or Legal. By default A4 is selected. The image is scaled to fit the page while maintaining its aspect ratio.
