    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is it really free?

    Yes, completely free. We keep the lights on through the ads displayed on the page — no premium tier, no paywall.

    Are my files uploaded to a server?

    No. All conversion happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device.

    Is there a file size limit?

    There's no hard limit, but very large files (above 100 MB) may be slow depending on your device.

    Can I convert multiple files at once?

    Yes! Select multiple files and they'll all be merged into a single PDF in the order shown.

    What browsers are supported?

    All modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, and Brave.
