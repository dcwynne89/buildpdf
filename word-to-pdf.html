Why Convert Word to PDF?

PDF is the universal document format — it looks identical on every device, can't be accidentally edited, and is accepted by virtually every employer, printer, school, and government form. Converting your Word document to PDF ensures:

  • Document layout and fonts are preserved exactly as you designed them.
  • The file can be opened on any device without Microsoft Office installed.
  • Recipients cannot accidentally edit or alter your document.
  • PDF is the standard format for resumes, contracts, reports, and official submissions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Microsoft Word or Office installed?

No. BuildPDF uses a browser-based DOCX parser that reads and converts Word documents without any Microsoft software. It works entirely in your browser.

Does my Word document get uploaded to a server?

Absolutely not. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your document data never leaves your device — not even temporarily.

Are all Word formatting features supported?

BuildPDF supports standard text formatting, headings, paragraphs, bold, italic, and basic layout. Complex elements like embedded charts or advanced macros may not render perfectly.

Is there a file size limit for DOCX files?

No hard limit. Files up to ~50 MB convert in seconds. Very large documents with many embedded images may take a few extra moments.

Can I convert .doc files (older Word format)?

BuildPDF currently supports the modern .docx format. For older .doc files, re-save them as .docx in Word, LibreOffice, or Google Docs first.
