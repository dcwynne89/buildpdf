Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Welcome to BuildPDF ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit buildpdf.co (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

BuildPDF is a 100% client-side application. All file conversion and extraction operations happen entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. Your files are never transmitted to our servers — we do not operate any server that receives, stores, or processes your files. We cannot access the contents of any file you convert on this Site.

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Netlify) and third-party advertising partners may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information when you visit the Site, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)

  • Referring URL (the page you came from)
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Pages viewed

This information is used in aggregate to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve user experience. It is not used to personally identify you.

This Site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to serve ads that are relevant to your interests, based on your browsing activity across websites.

Specifically:

  • DoubleClick cookies are used by Google to serve interest-based ads to users based on their browsing history.
  • Google may use the DART cookie to serve ads to users based on previous visits to this Site and other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad Settings page.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this Site or other sites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to BuildPDF and/or other sites on the internet. You can learn more about how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device by your browser. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Advertising: Google AdSense sets cookies to track ad impressions and clicks (see Section 3 above).

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the Site.

  • Right to access: You may request access to any personal data we hold about you.
